Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about reservations, discounts, dining policies, and general information about Cucina Restaurant & Pub.

General FAQs

Where is Cucina Restaurant & Pub located?

We are located at Asuoyeboah IPT, Kumasi.

Do you offer takeaway or online ordering?

Yes. Customers can order meals for takeaway, and online ordering options are also available for added convenience.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash and mobile money payments.

Is there a dress code?

Yes. We encourage neat and presentable dressing to match the dining atmosphere at Cucina. You can view full details on our Dresscode page.

Do you have drinks and non-alcoholic options?

Yes. Our menu includes a variety of refreshing drinks, juices, shakes, and other non-alcoholic options.

How can I contact the restaurant?

You can reach us by phone, email, or through the contact form on our Contact page.

Reservation Policy

Do I need a reservation before visiting?

Walk-ins are welcome, but reservations are recommended, especially during weekends, holidays, and peak evening hours.

How far in advance can I book a table?

You can place your reservation in advance to help us prepare for your visit and provide a smoother dining experience.

What happens if I arrive late for my reservation?

We try our best to hold reserved tables for a reasonable period. Arriving on time is encouraged to avoid delays or changes in seating availability.

Can I make a reservation for a group or special event?

Yes. Group reservations and special occasion bookings are accepted. You can include these details in the reservation form or contact us directly.

Discounts

Do you offer any discounts?

Yes. Cucina offers selected discounts on special occasions, as well as limited seasonal and promotional offers.

Is there a discount for birthdays or anniversaries?

Yes. Eligible special occasions may enjoy a 10% discount, depending on the current offer and reservation details.

Do discounts apply to every menu item?

Some discounts may apply only to selected meals, occasions, or promotional periods. Terms may vary depending on the offer available at the time.

Can I combine multiple discounts at once?

Discounts are generally not combined unless stated otherwise in a specific promotion.
